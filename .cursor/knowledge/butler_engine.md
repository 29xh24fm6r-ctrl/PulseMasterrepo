# Pulse Butler Engine

Pulse Butler handles:
- Email intelligence
- Relationship insights
- Weekly planning
- Task recommendations
- Notifications and digests
- Smart reminders
- Proactive suggestions

## Email Intelligence

- Thread tracking
- Contact scoring
- Lead/activity detection
- Relationship health indicators

## Relationship Engine

Tracks:
- Interactions
- Gaps in communication
- Sentiment changes

## Weekly Planning

Butler auto-generates:
- Weekly mission
- Priorities
- Energy allocation
- Review of last week
- Coaching notes
